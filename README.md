# Retail Sales Intelligence Dashboard (Tableau Visualization)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to visualize retail sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

It integrates insights derived from Excel (statistical analysis) and SQL (data aggregation) into a unified visual storytelling layer.

## Objectives

* Build an interactive business dashboard
* Visualize key performance indicators (KPIs)
* Analyze revenue trends and product performance
* Track order status and cancellation rates
* Enable exploratory business decision-making

## Dashboard Title

Retail Sales Intelligence: Orders, Revenue & Customer Insights

Dashboard Components

KPI Section

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Cancellation Rate

⸻

Visualizations

1. Revenue Trend Over Time

* Line chart of monthly revenue
* Helps identify seasonality patterns

2. Product Performance

* Bar chart of revenue by product
* Highlights top-performing products

3. Order Status Distribution

* Breakdown of completed vs cancelled vs pending orders

4. Payment Method Analysis

* Distribution of payment channels

5. Referral Source Insights

* Customer acquisition channels

## Key Insights

* Revenue is concentrated in a small number of high-performing products
* Most orders are successfully completed, with a low cancellation rate
* Online and referral-based channels drive significant customer acquisition
* Clear seasonal fluctuations observed in sales trends

## Tools Used

* Tableau Desktop
* Data extracted from MySQL
* Pre-cleaned Excel dataset

## Key Outcome

This dashboard provides a consolidated view of business performance, enabling stakeholders to quickly assess revenue health, operational efficiency, and customer behavior patterns.
